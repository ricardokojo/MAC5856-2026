This post is about my experience following tutorials [7](https://flusp.ime.usp.br/iio/iio-dummy-anatomy/) and [8](https://flusp.ime.usp.br/iio/experiment-one-iio-dummy/).

The seventh tutorial was actually an exploration of the IIO dummy module. Therefore, there was no action other than exploring the code. It was fairly interesting to see how it's organized, since I'm not used to C. Kinda hard to grasp everything, but I tried my best :)

For tutorial 8, things went smoothly. Also, I didn't understand what to do with `DUMMY_INDEX_SOFT_TIMESTAMP`, so I simply ignored it and everything still worked.

One thing that's worrying me is the building step, which takes at least 11 minutes to run. There's probably a better way to do it, but I'll find out in class talking to the TAs.
